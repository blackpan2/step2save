Steps2Save

Step to save money!

Navigate Wegmans in a fun new way with your friends and earn coupons as you shop.

Play Store
App Store

Features

Create a Shopping List

Select recipes, individual ingredients, or anything else and make yourself a list so you don't forget anything, even the cherry on top.

Earn Coupons

All you have to do is what you would normally, walking around the store will earn you coupons for things related to what you want to buy.

Shop with Friends

Is your roommate going to the store? Send them your grocery list. Find out about what recipes your friends recently made and try them for yourself.

Store Locator

If you aren't at your local Wegmans we'll help you find a store near you so you can get back to earning your coupons.

Community

Help impact your community in a positive way by sharing recipies and encouraging healthy habits! Compete for who earns the most coupons with their steps!

Stay Healthy

Find healthy recipies and alternatives with the help of our healthy suggestions powered by Google Cloud Platform's machine learning.
